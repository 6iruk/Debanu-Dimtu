DRIVE Project

Debanu Dimtu Trading PLC is committed to transforming Ethiopia's livestock industry through innovative projects that support resilience, inclusivity, and sustainable growth. One of our flagship initiatives is the DRIVE Project (De-risking, Inclusion, and Value Enhancement of Pastoral Economies), a major undertaking supported by the Ministry of Agriculture and financed by the World Bank. This project is a cornerstone in our vision to modernize the livestock supply chain, empower pastoral communities, and elevate Ethiopia's status as a leading exporter in the livestock sector.

Building on the DRIVE Project's foundation, Debenu Dimtu Trading PLC is committed to expanding our livestock infrastructure and partnerships. With continued support and investment, we aim to replicate this sustainable model in other regions, creating a scalable pathway for Ethiopia’s livestock sector to thrive on the global stage.

Reduce Risk and Increase Economic Stability

By providing pastoralists with reliable market access, stable pricing, and quality veterinary care, the project mitigates financial and health risks in the livestock industry.

Enhance Inclusion and Empower Local Communities

The project provides direct economic opportunities to pastoralists, creating 600 jobs with a focus on youth and women, and supporting a stable, fair income stream for community members.

Increase Export Capacity and Foreign Exchange Earnings

By supporting high-quality livestock production, the DRIVE Project aims to facilitate the export of 1,000,000 animals annually, boosting Ethiopia’s competitiveness in global markets and contributing to national economic growth.
